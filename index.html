Postdoctoral researcher in Hydrocephalus Proteomics

I’m a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Hydrocephalus Research Center, Indiana University (Indianapolis), researching phosphoproteome remodeling and ion channel regulation (TRPV4) in hydrocephalus. I earned my Ph.D. in Biochemistry from the University of Notre Dame (May 2024), where I investigated gene expression changes in Miller-Dieker Syndrome and the potential involvement of human METTL16.
